Syllabus for International Telenovela Got Talent Academy

Course Title: International Telenovela Got Talent Academy

Course Description

The International Telenovela Got Talent Academy offers a comprehensive program designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the field of international telenovela production. This syllabus covers novel writing, television drama series development, and pre and post-production content to prepare students for a successful career in the industry.

Course Outline

Module 1: Introduction to Telenovelas
- History and evolution of telenovelas
- Cultural influences on telenovelas
- Market trends and audience preferences

Module 2: Novel Writing for Telenovelas
- Understanding the telenovela genre
- Developing compelling characters and storylines
- Structuring episodes for maximum impact

Module 3: Television Drama Series Development
- Concept development and pitch creation
- Character development and arcs
- Plotting and pacing for a series format

Module 4: Pre-Production Process
- Script development and revisions
- Casting and character selection
- Location scouting and set design

Module 5: Production Phase
- Directing techniques for telenovelas
- Cinematography and lighting
- Working with actors and crew

Module 6: Post-Production and Editing
- Editing for telenovelas
- Sound design and music selection
- Special effects and CGI integration

Module 7: Marketing and Distribution
- Promotional strategies for telenovelas
- Securing distribution deals
- Building an international audience

Module 8: Final Project
- Students will work on a final project to develop a telenovela concept from script to final edit, incorporating all aspects of pre and post-production covered in the course.

Assessment

- Participation in class activities
- Scriptwriting assignments
- Project presentations
- Final project evaluation
